Who we are

NI NO is a private assistance service dedicated to helping people navigate the UK National Insurance Number application process. Our team has helped thousands of UK residents and newcomers successfully submit their National Insurance Number applications. We focus on making the process straightforward, accessible, and stress-free.

Why applicants choose NI NO

The official National Insurance Number application process requires you to complete an online form in English, print your documents, and sometimes manage phone calls with Jobcentre Plus. For many people — especially newcomers to the UK or those with limited English — this can be overwhelming.

NI NO was created to bridge that gap. We provide translation support, print your documents for you, check everything for errors, and handle appointment booking on your behalf. You focus on your life in the UK — we focus on your NINO application.

📌 Important to know: NI NO is a private assistance service. We are not affiliated with HMRC, Jobcentre Plus, or any UK government body. You can also apply directly through the official gov.uk website at no cost. We help those who want extra support — translation, document printing, error checking, and appointment management.

Common questions about NI NO

Is NI NO a government service?+
No. NI NO is a private assistance service. We are not affiliated with HMRC, Jobcentre Plus, or any UK government body. We help you prepare and submit your National Insurance Number application correctly.
Why would I use NI NO instead of applying directly?+
We help with language translation (70+ languages), print your documents, check for errors before submission, and manage Jobcentre appointments on your behalf. If you struggle with English or don't have a printer, our service makes the process much easier.
How long has NI NO been helping applicants?+
NI NO has been assisting UK residents with National Insurance Number applications for several years, helping thousands of people successfully get their NINO. Our team understands the process inside out.
Where is NI NO based?+
NI NO operates from the United Kingdom, supporting residents across England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland. Our support team works remotely to assist applicants nationwide.
Can NI NO guarantee I will get a National Insurance Number?+
No. Only HMRC can issue National Insurance Numbers. We help ensure your application is correct and submitted properly. If you meet the eligibility criteria and provide correct information, HMRC will process your application.
What types of applicants does NI NO help?+
We help UK citizens who never received their automatic NINO, international students, skilled workers on visas, EU citizens with settled status, dependants of visa holders, and anyone else with the right to work or study in the UK.
How do I contact NI NO if I have questions?+
You can email us at support@booknino.co.uk or enquiries@booknino.co.uk. We typically reply within 1-2 hours on weekdays. We don't offer phone support to keep response times fast and track all conversations.
What happens after I submit my application through NI NO?+
Our team reviews your application for errors, prints any required documents, and submits everything to HMRC. You'll receive email updates throughout the process. If an interview is needed, we book it for you.
