Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how NI NO uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. We believe in transparency about how we collect and use your information.

Last updated: June 2026 · Version 2.0

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, laptop, tablet, or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, enhance user experience, and provide information to website owners.

Cookies are not harmful — they cannot carry viruses or install malware on your device. They simply help us understand how visitors interact with our website so we can improve your experience.

How NI NO uses cookies

We use cookies for several legitimate purposes to make our website function properly and to understand how our visitors use our services. Specifically, we use cookies to:

  • Ensure our website works correctly and you can complete forms without issues
  • Remember your language preferences when you use our translation features
  • Understand how visitors navigate our site so we can improve content layout
  • Analyse website traffic and usage patterns to enhance performance
  • Prevent fraudulent activity and maintain website security

Types of cookies we use

Essential cookies (Strictly Necessary)

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation, form submissions, and access to secure areas. You cannot opt out of these cookies as the website would not work correctly without them.

Preference cookies (Functionality)

These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make, such as your language selection for our translation feature. They improve your experience by personalising content for you.

Analytics cookies (Performance)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about page visits, time spent on site, and any error messages. This helps us improve our website content and structure.

How to manage or delete cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to:

  • Notify you when you receive a cookie
  • Block all cookies from specific websites
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser
  • Block all third-party cookies

To manage cookies, check your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Site permissions → Cookies and site data
📌 Important: If you disable essential cookies, some parts of our website may not function correctly. Form submissions, language selection, and application progress may be affected.

Your cookie consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner asking for your consent to use non-essential cookies (analytics and preference cookies). Essential cookies are always active as they are necessary for the website to function.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the cookie settings link at the bottom of our pages. You can also clear existing cookies through your browser settings.

By continuing to use our website without changing your cookie settings, you agree to our use of essential cookies. For analytics and preference cookies, we require your explicit consent which you can provide or withdraw at any time.
