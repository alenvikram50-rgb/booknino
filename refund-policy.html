Refund Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

At NI NO ("we", "us", "our"), we strive to provide high-quality assistance for your National Insurance number needs. This Refund Policy explains when and how you may be eligible for a refund of our service fees. Please read this policy carefully before using our services.

Important Notice: You can apply for a National Insurance number for free directly through the official GOV.UK website. Our service fee is for the convenience of guided assistance and application checking. Refunds are subject to the terms below.

1. Our Service Fee

Our service fee is clearly displayed on our website before you submit your application. The fee covers:

  • Reviewing your application for completeness and errors
  • Providing guidance on required documents
  • Submitting your application to HMRC on your behalf
  • Tracking and delivery of your NI number confirmation letter

We do not charge any hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay.

2. General Refund Policy

Due to the nature of our services, we generally do not offer refunds once we have begun processing your application. However, we understand that exceptional circumstances may arise, and we will consider refund requests on a case-by-case basis.

Key Point: You may cancel your application and request a full refund within 24 hours of payment, provided we have not yet started processing your application.

3. When You May Be Eligible for a Refund

We may issue a refund in the following circumstances:

  • Cancellation within 24 hours: If you cancel your application within 24 hours of payment and we have not yet started processing your application, you will receive a full refund.
  • Duplicate payment: If you accidentally make a duplicate payment, we will refund the extra amount.
  • Technical error: If a technical error on our part prevents us from processing your application, you will receive a full refund.
  • Service not delivered: If we fail to deliver the service as described within a reasonable timeframe, you may be eligible for a partial or full refund at our discretion.

4. When Refunds Are Not Available

Refunds will not be issued in the following situations:

  • After we have started processing your application (typically within 24 hours of payment)
  • If your application is rejected by HMRC due to information you provided being inaccurate or incomplete
  • If you change your mind after we have already submitted your application
  • If you apply for a National Insurance number but are not eligible (e.g., you already have one, you are under 16, or you do not have the right to work in the UK)
  • If there are delays caused by HMRC or postal services (Royal Mail)
  • If you fail to provide required documents or respond to our requests for information
Important: HMRC makes the final decision on all National Insurance number applications. We cannot influence their decision, and we do not offer refunds solely because your application was rejected by HMRC.

5. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund, please follow these steps:

Refund Request Process

  1. Contact us at refunds@nino.co.uk within 14 days of your payment
  2. Include your full name, email address used for the application, and order/reference number
  3. Clearly explain the reason for your refund request
  4. Attach any supporting documentation (if applicable)
  5. We will acknowledge your request within 2 working days
  6. We will review your request and respond within 10 working days

6. Processing Refunds

If your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed using the original payment method used for the transaction
  • Please allow 5-10 working days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been processed
  • Refunds will be issued in British Pounds (GBP) for the exact amount you paid

7. Cancellation Rights (Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013)

Under the Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013, you have the right to cancel your order within 14 days without giving any reason. However, this right does not apply once we have begun providing the service with your explicit consent.

By completing your application and making payment, you explicitly consent to us beginning the service immediately. If you wish to cancel, you must do so before we start processing your application. The easiest way to cancel is to email us at refunds@nino.co.uk within 24 hours of payment.

Your Statutory Rights: Nothing in this Refund Policy affects your statutory rights as a consumer under UK law, including your right to cancel under the Consumer Contracts Regulations.

8. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

If you dispute a payment with your bank or card issuer (chargeback) without first contacting us to request a refund, we reserve the right to:

  • Provide evidence of the service we provided to your bank
  • Suspend or terminate your access to our services
  • Refuse future service requests from you

We encourage you to contact us directly before initiating any chargeback, as we are committed to resolving any legitimate concerns you may have.

9. Changes to This Refund Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Changes will apply to applications submitted after the effective date of the change.
