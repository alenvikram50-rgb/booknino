What if you don't have standard documents?

If you cannot provide a passport, BRP, or UK birth certificate, HMRC allows alternative combinations. For example: expired passport + marriage certificate, or employer letter + council tax statement. NI NO consultants can advise based on your individual case — just email us your situation.

Frequently asked questions about documents

Do I need a passport to apply for National Insurance Number?+
Yes, a valid passport is the most common document. If you don't have a passport, you can use a BRP or UK birth certificate instead.
Can I apply without a BRP?+
Yes, you can use a valid passport or UK birth certificate instead of a BRP. EU citizens may use their national passport.
Do I need to send original documents?+
No, you only need to send digital copies (photos or scans). NI NO handles printing and forwarding to HMRC.
What if I don't have any identity documents?+
You may need to provide alternative documents like marriage certificate, birth certificate of child, or a letter from your employer confirming your identity.
How long are documents valid for NI application?+
Passports and BRPs should be valid at time of application. Expired documents are generally not accepted unless paired with another valid ID.
Do I need proof of address for NI Number?+
Yes, HMRC requires proof of UK residential address — a utility bill, bank statement, or tenancy agreement from the last 3 months works.
Can I use digital copies of my documents?+
Absolutely. Clear photos or scans are accepted. NI NO checks them for quality and prints everything for you.
What documents do EU citizens need?+
EU citizens need a valid passport and proof of right to work or study in the UK (settled status, visa, or employment contract).
Can I apply if I only have a driving licence?+
A UK driving licence alone is usually not sufficient for identity, but it can be used as supporting document alongside a passport or BRP.
How do I send my documents to NI NO?+
After completing our online form, you'll receive instructions to upload photos or scans. Everything is encrypted and handled securely.
